Evaluation Index

Enthusiastic Service

Participate in social care services and training for at least 2 hours during the semester.

Love For knowledge

Those who have completed at least any of the following in each semester can pass this standard:

－Borrow 10 volumes of library books.

－Read recommended books and complete 1 piece of experience writing.

－Participate in an event organized by the library.

－Participate in an event organized by the Counseling and Counseling Center.

－Participate in an event organized by Zhonghua Academy.

Sound Personality

Each semester has a performance score of 85 points or more, and participates in at least one activity organized by the Life Counseling Team of the Office of Academic Affairs, or is scored by the personality building, with a score of 10 points or more.

Love For knowledge

Every semester, those who have completed at least any of the following items in the school can pass this standard:

-Participate in an event organized by the health care team.

－Participate in physical fitness test.

－Participate in swimming test.

-Participate in sports activities at least 2 times.

Artistic taste

Participate in at least one school gardening and cultural exhibition (including the opening tea party) or one art and cultural activity (for example: music, film exhibition, speech, study, forum, design competition, etc.) every semester.

Sunshine Youth Certification

During the four years (eight semesters) of university at the school, anyone who has passed the index standards of "Enthusiastic Service", "Awareness and Knowledge", "Strong Physique", "Sound Personality" and "Art Taste" in that semester, and any of the items If the score is not zero, you will obtain the Sunshine Youth Certification for the semester.

Sunshine Youth Selection

Anyone who has an average score of 65 points for the five major evaluation indicators in the semester (any indicator cannot be 0 points), an academic average score of 65 points or more, and a practicability score of 85 points or more, will be entered into the final election.

The students who enter the top 50 of the school by points evaluation and become the sunshine youth of the semester will receive a total scholarship of 150,000 yuan and free use of the school’s sports and leisure center (gym) and indoor heated swimming pool. They can also apply for the student dormitory first in the next academic year.

Sports and leisure center

Chung Hwa University has a sports and leisure center in the student dormitory area. The center is equipped with cardiopulmonary fitness training area, muscle fitness training area, stretching room, VIP storage space and toilet. At the same time, it provides the latest sports equipment and diversified facilities. Oxygen courses, and the most professional staff will provide you with the most immediate service.

Indoor Heated Swimming Pool

The indoor heated swimming pool of Chung Hwa University is located on the fourth floor of the second building of the project. It is not a common floor-standing swimming pool, but is one of the best well-equipped swimming pools in Hsinchu City. The swimming pool is equipped with a fully automatic circulating filtration and sterilization system. The water quality is pure, so that users can feel at ease. The warm water is open all year round.

Engineering Hall One

Internet of Things and Mass Data Teaching Center

Information technology such as the Internet of Things (IoT) and Big Data is driving the development of Industry 4.0.

The Internet of Things and Mass Data Teaching Center of Chung Hwa University is a cross-college integrated classroom, which provides relevant teaching and research in various colleges, so as to have the ability to develop in line with industry trends. Current teaching and research include: tourism Internet of things, environmental control Internet of things, security Internet of things and other fields.

Management Hall One

Case Teaching Center

The school introduces the discussion-style case study emphasized by Harvard University, and the case classroom with a horseshoe theater structure allows students to experience and use the same level of international teaching equipment as Harvard University even though they are in China.

Through case teaching, students are trained to communicate and discuss face-to-face while also exhibiting the logic of problem-solving methods, so that students have an international outlook, have the courage to innovate and have more enthusiasm for learning.

Interactive Learning Innovation Teaching Center

The school promotes innovative teaching and combines the concepts of interactive learning, team-oriented learning and problem-oriented learning to create an interactive learning and innovative teaching center. Teachers and students can actively participate in discussions and subvert through the i-Learning digital learning system in the classroom. The traditional teaching method in the past.

The special desk and chair design in the classroom can be adjusted at any time according to the teaching method to enhance the learning effect.

Japanese Culture Classroom

The Japanese culture classroom is equipped with the country’s first interactive whiteboard, satellite synchronous reception and other technological teaching and cultural context teaching. The Japanese traditional Japanese room space in the classroom not only allows students to understand the essence of Japanese culture, but also provides students with experience in kimono dressing and tea ceremony. , Ikebana course.

The large mirror wall in the classroom is also used for graduation performance rehearsals and Japanese dance practice for applied Japanese students.

R&D Building

AI+ Experience Center

The first AI+ Center in Taiwan was completed at Chung Hwa University, providing students with more practical learning situations.

Chung Hwa University adds AI artificial intelligence to the majors of various colleges and departments, allowing students to use machine learning, face recognition and dialogue robots to apply three major AI technologies through experience, learning and practice, combining what they have learned to inspire students For the unlimited imagination of AI and the potential of innovative AI applications, cultivate cross-disciplinary talents.

Tuzi Building

Career Centered On You

Chung Hua University cooperated with the Labor Development Agency of the Ministry of Labor to set up a job center (Jia Taole Theme Learning Branch) in the lobby of the Tuzi Building to provide students with job search consultation and guidance.

The center is equipped with a workplace experience area, a self-service mock interview room, a resume preparation area, career consultation, a makeup area, and a seminar room. Each area uses information interactive methods to allow students to use the resources in the library for career exploration.

DOIT Studio

In order to realize the value of the innovative "DO IT Learning by Doing" that is co-constructed by design creativity and technological invention, DOIT Studio is built in the school to provide students with a space for hands-on creativity, so as to cultivate talents with both creative planning and conception capabilities.

DOIT Studio provides woodworking factories, laser cutting machines, 3D printers and other equipment, so that students can realize their dreams of productizing creative ideas.

Tab 6

Parcel mail collection

Mail Room

Students shuttle to the classroom and often fail to pick up the goods in time with the delivery time of the logistics company, causing both students and the logistics company to be troubled. In order to provide students with convenient parcel and mail collection services, the school has a parcel and mail collection service in the general affairs office's mail room. Students can pick up parcels from the service counter during working hours.

I Mailbox, Smart Station

The special life style of students traveling between classrooms, dormitories and even working places often causes logistics to face the trouble of repeated delivery because they cannot communicate smoothly. The school cooperates with the Industrial Technology Research Institute and Chunghwa Post to provide smart pick-up stations and i-mail services on campus, and provide students with parcel collection services 24 hours a day, all year round.

I Mailbox, Smart Station

In addition to entering the campus of Chung Hua University, Chunghwa Post also has 6 ATMs on campus, providing high-quality, convenient, fast deposit and parcel services for teachers and students of the school.

24-Hour Automatic Service Machine

Graduating alumni and current students often need to apply for documentary proofs for data payment. The school has a 24-hour automatic server machine in the lobby of the administrative building. All services such as air-conditioning storage, transcript application, parking permit payment application and other services can be processed by the server , And get help or get documents immediately.
