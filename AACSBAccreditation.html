About AACSB


• The Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB) is one of the most authoritative professional organizations for the accreditation of business and management schools. The mission of AACSB is to foster engagement, accelerate innovation, and amplify impact in business education. Currently there are 840 accredited business schools worldwide, and 1,700 member organizations.
• CHU College of Management earned its initial accreditation of AACSB in August, 2016. The accreditation is important for meeting international standards and assuring quality of business education offered by the College. AACSB accreditation is the most important indicator of meeting international standards, and. It continually aims to provide a good opportunity for the students, and teachers for continuous improvement.

Our Mission

Our Strength’s
