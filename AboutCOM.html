History

CHU was successfully established in 1990 by a group of influential entrepreneurs in the suburban region of Hsinchu County. With the main objective to inspire and facilitate quality education, promote the advancement of local industries and to foster regional prosperity has been the driving force for university’s success. It was formerly known as Chung Hua Polytechnic Institute before it was upgraded to its current status in 1997. At present, the University has five colleges comprising of College of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Management, Architecture & Planning, Humanities & Social Sciences, and Tourism. The total strength of the students enrolled in the university is more than 6500 students and well supported by more than 230 teaching faculties across varies degree programs and colleges of CHU.
The COM was established in the year 1997. Till date, The COM has more than 1004 students with 64 full-time faculties.
